The Moment You Need To Remove Garlic From The Menu

When it's time to remove garlic from a dish or menu, it's often due to its overpowering flavor dominating other ingredients or when catering to individuals with garlic allergies or sensitivities. Removing garlic can be necessary to accommodate diverse dietary preferences or to ensure a milder flavor profile, especially in dishes where garlic plays a supporting role rather than being the star ingredient. Additionally, some people may prefer to avoid garlic for personal or cultural reasons. Substituting garlic with alternative flavorings like herbs, spices, or garlic-infused oils can maintain depth of flavor while catering to different preferences or dietary needs, ensuring that the dish remains enjoyable for all.
